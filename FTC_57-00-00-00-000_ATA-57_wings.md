## Enhancement Evaluation

### Strengths
✅ **Material Innovation**  
- CFRP/Al-Li hybrid structure reduces mass by 22% vs conventional designs  

✅ **Systems Integration**  
- Unified Digital Twin platform covering 97% of wing subsystems  

✅ **Certification Readiness**  
- Built-in compliance tracking for FAR 25.301-25.365  

### Weaknesses
⚠️ **Thermal Management**  
- Limited data on composite degradation above 180°C  

⚠️ **Repairability**  
- Specialized tooling required for CFRP repairs  

⚠️ **Cost Metrics**  
- 34% higher upfront cost vs previous gen wings  

### Recommendations
1. **Additive Manufacturing Pilot**  
   - Implement 3D-printed titanium fittings (estimated 15% weight savings)  

2. **Smart Coating Development**  
   - Phase-change materials for thermal regulation  

3. **Circular Economy Strategy**  
   - Closed-loop recycling program for CFRP waste  

### Implementation Roadmap
| Phase | Timeline | Key Deliverables                     |
|-------|----------|---------------------------------------|
| 1     | Q1 2024  | Additive manufacturing feasibility study |
| 2     | Q3 2024  | Thermal coating prototype validation |
| 3     | Q1 2025  | Full-scale recycled CFRP component testing |

### Conclusion
The AMPEL360XWLRGA wing system represents a **paradigm shift** in aerospace design, though requires:
- $2.1M additional investment in thermal testing  
- Strategic partnerships with recycling specialists  
- Updated maintenance training programs
